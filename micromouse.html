Micromouse

Robotics • 2015  |  C++ · Arduino · PID · A*

Our **Micromouse** robot claimed first place at the 2015 UH Micromouse competition, solving a 16×16 maze in under 8 seconds. Highlights include a custom STM32-based PCB, dual-core PID motor control, incremental encoders, and an A* path-planning algorithm executed on-board in C++.

I handled control-loop tuning and real-time sensor fusion, achieving line-following error < 1 mm at 1 m/s. The chassis was 3-D printed in ABS and weighed only 120 g.
